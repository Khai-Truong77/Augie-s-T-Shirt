Learn About Us


Augie Garcia began designing custom t-shirts in 2015. Since then she has expanded her business to incorporate the latest technology in order to create the best results for any theme. In 2020, Augie purchased a Direct-To-Garment printer, allowing her to create a vast catalog of images that can be imprinted on t-shirts and other garments in full color and stunning tones. Augie has a background in graphical design and graduated from the University of Georgia. Her favorites things to are color coordination, hiking, swimming and reading. All of her customers have said that created an easy to use site to create sentimental shirts for any occassion.









Richard Petty joined Augie's Custom T-Shirts in 2019 as the Bussiness Manager. He graduated from the University of Georgia back in 1985 with a degree in Bussiness. Richard had always loved seeing bright faces from his old alma mater. He dedicated his career to helping passionate alumni path their futures. He has shares in over 20 different start ups with nearly all of them being started from his home town of Atlanta. Richard enjoys spending his time with his family, kayaking, and traveling. As a man of bussiness, Richard saw the potential successs of Augie's vision and hopped on the board to bring what he says "An Unforgettable Service" to Middle Georgia.
